# Practice Areas (Services)

Comprehensive legal support for all family matters:

1.  **Divorce & Legal Separation**: Contested and uncontested divorce, asset division, spousal support, and guidance through Texas community property laws.
2.  **Child Custody & Visitation**: Sole and joint custody arrangements, parenting plans, visitation schedules, and relocation disputes.
3.  **Child Support**: Calculating, establishing, and modifying child support orders in accordance with Texas Family Code guidelines.
4.  **Property Division**: Fair division of marital assets, real estate, retirement accounts, businesses, and debt under Texas community property rules.
5.  **Spousal Support / Alimony**: Negotiating and litigating spousal maintenance, including temporary and post-divorce support.
6.  **Adoption**: Stepparent adoption, private domestic adoption, and termination of parental rights proceedings.
7.  **Protective Orders**: Emergency and long-term protective orders for victims of domestic violence or harassment.
8.  **Paternity**: Establishing or challenging paternity, father's rights, and legal parentage disputes.
9.  **Prenuptial & Postnuptial Agreements**: Drafting and reviewing marital agreements that protect both parties' interests.
10. **Modifications & Enforcement**: Modifying existing court orders and enforcing compliance when the other party fails to follow them.
