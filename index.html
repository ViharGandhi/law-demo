Our Practice Areas

Comprehensive legal support for all family matters.

Divorce & Legal Separation

Contested and uncontested divorce, asset division, spousal support, and guidance through Texas community property laws.

Child Custody & Visitation

Sole and joint custody arrangements, parenting plans, visitation schedules, and relocation disputes.

Child Support

Calculating, establishing, and modifying child support orders in accordance with Texas Family Code guidelines.

Property Division

Fair division of marital assets, real estate, retirement accounts, businesses, and debt under Texas community property rules.

Spousal Support / Alimony

Negotiating and litigating spousal maintenance, including temporary and post-divorce support.

Adoption

Stepparent adoption, private domestic adoption, and termination of parental rights proceedings.

Protective Orders

Emergency and long-term protective orders for victims of domestic violence or harassment.

Paternity

Establishing or challenging paternity, father's rights, and legal parentage disputes.

Prenuptial & Postnuptial

Drafting and reviewing marital agreements that protect both parties' interests.

Modifications & Enforcement

Modifying existing court orders and enforcing compliance when the other party fails to follow them.
